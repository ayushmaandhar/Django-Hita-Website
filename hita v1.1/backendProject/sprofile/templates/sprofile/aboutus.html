Our Vision

There has not been any material change to the Legal services sector for many years, despite the surrounding digital evolution. As a result, the Legal services sector is falling behind and is no longer as relevant to the consumer which will continue to erode without meaningful change.
  Hita intends to disrupt the Legal services sector by incorporating digitalization as a core tenant of the industry. We will leverage a marketplace model that’s two-sided (1. The consumer, 2. The legal services personnel). The consumer will simply be matched to legal services personnel based on their legal needs as inputted (or instruction) by the consumer. The legal services personnel will manage the relationship with the consumer end to end with no middle step that involves the entity (Legal Services Marketplace) to create the necessary scalability and efficiency of the service.

Our Values

Hita will provide the consumer transparency throughout their legal services journey, leveraging a digital self-service platform. The legal services personnel will be under contract with Legal Services Marketplace when fulfilling legal services on the Legal Services Marketplace platform.
